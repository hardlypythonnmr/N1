## Key Findings

- Purpose in life = **17% reduced all-cause mortality** (136,265 participants)
- Career adaptability (CAAS): **149 studies, 82,519 participants** — no consumer tool exists
- ACT values interventions: effect sizes **g = 0.57-0.82** (large)
- SDT-based career chatbot **outperformed generic chatbot** (CHI 2025)
- **91% of people experience purpose anxiety**
- Only **3-5% of career changers access professional counseling**

## The Product Thesis (What Does Not Exist)

A tool that:

1. **Measures** using validated instruments (CAAS, MLQ, VIA, Big Five, SDT needs, Ryff)
2. **Intervenes** using evidence-based exercises (ACT values, Odyssey Plans, crafting experiments)
3. **Tracks** longitudinal progress (re-measurement, alignment scores)
4. **Integrates** frameworks into coherent career self-knowledge profile
5. **Cites** every recommendation to a specific study

## Validated Tools Nobody Has Productized

1. CAAS-based career adaptability program (149 studies, no consumer tool)
2. SDT need-satisfaction diagnostic for career decisions
3. ACT values-to-career alignment tracker
4. Narrative identity construction platform (Savickas + Ibarra)
5. Self-efficacy mastery experience tracker (Bandura)
6. Flow-activity mapping for career fit
7. MLQ + CAAS + Ryff combined profile (30 items total)

## Assessment Tiers

**Tier 1 (Strong):** Big Five, VIA Character Strengths, Hogan, CliftonStrengths, CAAS
**Tier 2 (Moderate):** 16PF, Schwartz Values, Career Anchors, MLQ
**Tier 3 (Weak):** MBTI (50% get different type on retest), Enneagram, DISC
