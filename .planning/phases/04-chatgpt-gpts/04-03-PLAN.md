**gpts/compass/knowledge/caas-guide.md**:
CAAS-12 theoretical background for use when the GPT needs to explain subscale scores:

- What CAAS-12 measures (career adaptability theory — Savickas & Porfeli, 2012)
- Each subscale with deeper interpretation:
  - Concern: future orientation, planning behavior
  - Control: personal agency (LATAM calibration: collectivist cultures run lower, 2-3 normative)
  - Curiosity: exploration of possible selves and options
  - Confidence: career decision self-efficacy
- Interpretation table: 1-2 = low; 3 = moderate; 4-5 = high — with 1-line interpretation + recommended next step for each
- Note: "This GPT administers a conversational approximation, not the validated 12-item Likert scale. For clinical/research use, direct users to the standardized instrument."
